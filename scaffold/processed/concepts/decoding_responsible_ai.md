# About this module

The algorithms that determine what news you see, who gets a loan, and even who lands a job interview are making decisions that profoundly impact human lives every day. Behind this increasingly powerful AI lies a critical question: Just because people can build it, should they? And if they do, how will they be sure AI benefits humanity rather than harms it?

Welcome to the *Decoding Responsible AI: Upholding Pillars of Trust* module! In this module, you’ll explore the ethical dimensions of AI and how they shape our technological landscape. You’ll learn how to analyze AI ethics challenges in common AI applications, evaluate fairness and transparency in AI tools, and assess the AI ethics implications of AI-generated outputs. This informed approach will equip you to make responsible decisions about designing, implementing, and using AI tools and applications that earn trust and respect human values.
